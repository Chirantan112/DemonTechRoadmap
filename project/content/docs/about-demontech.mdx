# About DemonTech

DemonTech is an open-source initiative dedicated to simplifying the developer journey. We maintain an ecosystem of interactive, highly detailed roadmaps that guide developers from complete beginner to industry expert.

## Overview
Founded out of frustration with the chaotic state of tech education, DemonTech organizes the chaos. Instead of spending hours searching for the "best" tutorial, learners can follow our curated, opinionated paths. We gather the best free resources on the internet and sequence them logically.

## Project Goals
1. **Eliminate Decision Fatigue**: Provide a single, definitive path for learning a specific technology.
2. **Standardize Knowledge**: Ensure learners aren't skipping critical fundamentals (like Git or basic networking) before jumping into advanced frameworks.
3. **Bridge the Gap**: Help learners transition from simple coding exercises to building production-ready architectures.

## Why Demon Tech Exists
The internet is flooded with coding tutorials, yet the drop-out rate for self-taught developers is staggering. Why? Because tutorials teach syntax, but they rarely teach context. Demon Tech exists to provide that missing context. We show you *why* you need to learn a tool, *when* to use it, and *how* it connects to the broader ecosystem.

## Platform Features
- **Interactive Nodes**: Track your progress by marking topics as "in progress" or "completed."
- **Curated Resources**: Every node contains hand-picked free resources (articles, videos, documentation).
- **Project Milestones**: Regular checkpoints require you to build real projects before moving on.
- **Local Storage Sync**: Your progress is securely saved in your browser, so you never lose your place.

## Community Values
- **Inclusivity**: We welcome learners of all levels. There are no "stupid" questions here.
- **Quality**: We rigorously review all recommended resources to ensure they are up-to-date and accurate.
- **Transparency**: Our development process is open. Anyone can see our roadmap for the platform itself.

## Future Direction
We are actively working on building collaborative features. In the future, you'll be able to create accounts to sync your progress across devices, form study groups, and showcase your completed projects directly on a DemonTech portfolio profile.
